2. Events and event propagation

In this example we look at event propagation. When you click anywhere in this box, independently on the cursor style, the box will toggle between grey and white. The same thing happens when you click on the image, and in addition, will the image toggle between larger and smaller size.

Any click in the image is also a click in the box which has its own eventhandler, the event is propagated up through the chain of eventhandlers. This behaviour can be stopped by using the method stopPropagation().
